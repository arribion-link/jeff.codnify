Dev Automation

Cut down repetitive tasks and focus on shipping.

Codnify simplifies your everyday engineering routines with configurable pipelines, prebuilt scripts, and smart triggers. Whether it’s linting, testing, or deployment—Codnify ensures these tasks run in the background while you focus on creating value.

Smart Analytics

Understand how your codebase evolves.

Get actionable insights into code health, review cycles, test coverage, and commit velocity. Codnify visualizes trends over time, pinpoints risky files, and offers data-driven suggestions to boost code quality and team velocity.

Collaboration Tools

Stay in sync with your team and tools.

Workflows stay aligned across distributed teams using inline discussions, real-time notifications, team tagging, and progress boards. No more jumping between platforms—Codnify is your centralized dev hub.

Plugins & Integrations

Connect with Git, CI/CD, IDEs, and more.

Extend Codnify to fit your ecosystem. Native support for GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Jenkins, CircleCI, Slack, VS Code, and more. Build your own integrations or activate prebuilt ones with a single click.

Custom Dashboards

Make your dev data work for you.

Drag-and-drop widgets for productivity tracking, pull request health, deployment status, and user-defined KPIs. Every developer and team can craft their own performance view.
